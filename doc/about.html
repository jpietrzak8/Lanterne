Lanterne

An app for experimenting with the flash LEDs

Version 0.5.0

Lanterne is a tiny app for messing around with the LEDs used by the N900 to provide a flash for the camera. If there is interest, it may grow into something more...

Lanterne is a free and open-source program! Use it however you like. But if you wish to send a few appreciative dollars my way, I do accept donations. All funds received will be put towards the purchase of another N900 for development work. Thank you!
