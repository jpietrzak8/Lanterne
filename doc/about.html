Lanterne

An all-purpose flashlight app

Version 0.5.11

Lanterne is a small app that provides a handful of ways to use the N900's camera flash and indicator LEDs. Standard "torch" usage is available, as well as a continuous pulse, an SOS signal, and a general-purpose Morse code transmitter.

Lanterne is a free and open-source program! Use it however you like. But if you wish to send a few appreciative dollars my way, I do accept donations. All funds received will be put towards the purchase of another N900 for development work. Thank you!
